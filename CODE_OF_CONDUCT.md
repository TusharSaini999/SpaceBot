# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We, as contributors and maintainers of **SpaceBOT ISRO – AI Assistant**, pledge to make participation in our community a respectful, inclusive, and harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, gender, identity, experience, nationality, or background.

We are committed to fostering a welcoming and supportive environment for all.

---

## Our Standards

We encourage the following behaviors:

✅ Being respectful, kind, and considerate  
✅ Using inclusive and professional language  
✅ Providing constructive feedback  
✅ Supporting and encouraging fellow contributors  
✅ Accepting responsibility for mistakes and learning from them  

Unacceptable behaviors include:

❌ Harassment, discrimination, or hate speech  
❌ Personal attacks or insulting remarks  
❌ Trolling, spamming, or disruptive behavior  
❌ Publishing private information without consent  
❌ Any conduct that harms the community  

---

## Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for:

- Clarifying and enforcing community standards  
- Taking appropriate action against unacceptable behavior  
- Ensuring a safe and productive environment  

Maintainers have the right to remove, edit, or reject contributions that do not align with this Code of Conduct.

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:

- All project spaces (GitHub repositories, issues, pull requests, discussions)
- All official communication channels
- All community interactions related to this project

---

## Enforcement

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it to the project maintainer:

📧 **Contact:** Tushar Saini (Project Owner)

All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly. Confidentiality will be maintained where possible.

---

## Enforcement Guidelines

Community leaders may take the following actions depending on the severity:

1. **Warning** – A private reminder of community standards  
2. **Temporary Ban** – Temporary removal from participation  
3. **Permanent Ban** – Permanent removal from the project
